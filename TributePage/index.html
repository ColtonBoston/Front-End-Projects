Steve Irwin's life:

  • 1962 - Born in Essendon, Victoria, Australia.
  • 1970 - Moved with his parents to Queensland, where he attended Landsborough State School and Caloundra State High School.
  • 1971 - Wrestled his first crocodile at age 9.
  • 1991 - Took over management of the Queensland Reptile and Fauna Park, which he later renamed Australia Zoo.
  • 1992 - Married his wife, Terri Raines.
  • 1996 - Debut of his TV series, The Crocodile Hunter.
  • 1997 - Discovered a new species of turtle, which he named Irwin's Turtle, while on a fishing trip on the coast of Queensland with his father.
  • 1998 - His daughter, Bindi Sue Irwin, was born.
  • 2001 - Awarded the Centenary Medal by the Australian government.
  • 2003 - His son, Robert Clarence "Bob" Irwin, was born.
  • 2006 - Died at the age of 44 due to a stingray injury to the heart.
